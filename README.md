This project showcases an interactive E-Commerce Sales Dashboard built using Power BI for analyzing business performance across products, regions, and time periods.

# Key Features & Insights

- KPIs Displayed: Total Sales Amount (438K), Total Profit (37K), Total Quantity Sold (5615), Average Order Value (121K)

- Profit Trend by Month: Visualizes profitable and loss months to support strategic planning

- Sales & Profit by Region: Compares state-wise performance such as Maharashtra, Madhya Pradesh, and others

- Category Analysis: Shows contribution of major product categories like Clothing (63%), Electronics (21%), and Furniture (17%)

- Payment Mode Analysis: Highlights customer purchasing trends with COD as the most used mode (44%)

- Sub-Category Performance: Identifies top profit-generating segments

- Filters: Quarter and State filters for detailed drill-down insights

# Objective

To help business stakeholders understand sales behavior, identify high-profit months, and make informed decisions to improve revenue and market expansion.

# Tools & Skills Used

- Power BI

- Data Modeling & DAX

- Data Cleaning & Transformation (Power Query)

- Interactive Dashboard Development

- Business Intelligence & Data Visualization

# Outcome

- The dashboard provides clear visibility into business performance, enabling decisions on:

- Improving weak-performing product categories

- Optimizing regional sales strategies

- Enhancing payment mode offerings based on customer preference
